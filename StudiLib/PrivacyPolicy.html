Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

We at StudiLib respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use StudiLib.

1. Information We Collect

By using Unity Player Accounts, we minimize the data we store directly. We collect:

  • Email Address: Used strictly for One-Time Password (OTP) login codes and account verification.
  • Player ID: A unique string generated by Unity to keep your save data linked to you.
  • Technical Data: Basic device info (OS, IP address) processed by Unity for security and stability.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used specifically to:

  • Authenticate your login sessions.
  • Protect your game progress from unauthorized access (preventing data leaks).
  • Enable cross-device synchronization of your player profile.

3. Third-Party Processing (Unity)

Our backend is powered by Unity Gaming Services (UGS). Your login and data storage are managed by them. For more details on how they secure your info, please visit the Unity Privacy Policy.

4. Data Deletion & Your Rights

You have the right to access or delete your data at any time. Warning: Deleting your account will permanently erase all progress, items, and settings.

To request deletion, please contact us at the email below or use the "Delete Account" button in the game settings.
