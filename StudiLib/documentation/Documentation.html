About us:

We are a team of two people who are working on the development of StudiLib. Our goal is to create a platform that helps students study more effectively and collaborate with their peers.

Candy (She/Her) - Lead Developer & Designer

Candy is the lead developer and designer of StudiLib. She is responsible for the overall vision and direction of the project, as well as the user interface, experience design and all the backend stuff.

Ashmita (She/Her) - Artist & Illustrator

Ashmita is the artist and illustrator of StudiLib. She is responsible for the visual design, character creation, and overall aesthetic of the application, as well as social media post design.
